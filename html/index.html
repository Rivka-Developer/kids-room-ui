About Our Shop

The History Bring It Home

One house, a whole world of design. Much more than a store - it is part of a group of brands born from a love of design and precise craftsmanship. Behind all this are Shai Lugsi and Stav Brin - people who live and breathe design, who invite you to choose correctly and design smartly. • BRING WEAR - our fashion brand, with everyday items with a clean and precise aesthetic. • "Crazy about Nordic design" - a community with tens of thousands of members who understand that what is simple - is the most beautiful. • Crazy Nordic and Walls - leading magazines in Israel in the fields of design, inspiration and lifestyle. • Pickinteri - the place to find the right professionals for your project. • Studio Crazy - our interior design studio, which leads Nordic projects throughout the country.

Personal Setting

Watches

Wall clocks are a decorative item on the wall, and they are another layer in completing the work of space design. At Bering, we have created a diverse collection of clocks characterized by minimalist, clean lines and a color palette that will allow you to choose a wall clock that matches the style and color palette of the space.

BRING WEAR. When clothes feel like home

From day one, BRING was born out of a love for precise design, quiet colors, and a life that is pleasant to live in. We started with furniture – because we believed that every home should feel precise, calm, and practical. But over time, we realized that it didn’t end there. BRING WEAR is the natural continuation – an Israeli fashion brand born from the same DNA of design. If you connected with what BRING brings to the home – you will probably also love what we bring to the closet.

Children's room design

Nice to meet you, a collection of children's rooms designed from solid wood, with a clean and functional design that will give children time to learn, play and create, and turn the room into a place they want to stay in. We offer children's room design using shelves, racks, wall lamps, and a large variety of designed wall stickers that will decorate the wall and give it an additional dimension of color and shape.
